Section 01

Abstract

Blood availability is a critical factor in saving lives during medical emergencies. In Gwadar, the absence of a centralized and reliable blood management system results in delays in locating suitable donors. Existing methods rely on personal networks and social media, which are inefficient and unstructured.

This project proposes the development of a hospital-centric blood donation system that connects hospitals and donors through a mobile application. Hospitals can post verified blood requests, and the system identifies and notifies nearby eligible donors using location-based services.

The system also includes an Emergency Broadcast feature for urgent cases, distance calculation between donors and hospitals, and real-time hospital blood demand tracking. Additionally, an Artificial Intelligence (AI)-based demand prediction module is incorporated to forecast future blood requirements based on historical hospital data.

Section 02

Introduction

The Challenge

Timely access to blood is essential in emergency healthcare. In Gwadar, the lack of a structured blood donation system leads to delays that can risk patient lives. Hospitals often struggle to quickly find donors, especially for rare blood groups.

Our Solution

A smart hospital-driven blood donation system that ensures reliability, efficiency, and speed. By integrating real-time notifications, map-based location tracking, and AI-based predictions, the system will significantly improve emergency response and blood management in Gwadar.

Section 06

Proposed System

The proposed system consists of a mobile application and backend server designed to connect hospitals and donors efficiently.

Hospitals Can

Register and log in
Post blood requirements (blood group, units, urgency)
Trigger emergency broadcast alerts

Donors Can

Register with blood group, phone number, and location
View nearby hospital requests
See distance from hospital
Receive real-time notifications
Respond to emergency requests

AI Intelligence Module

The system will display all hospitals in Gwadar on a map, calculate distance between donors and hospitals, and highlight which hospitals currently need blood. An AI module will analyze historical hospital request data to predict future demand trends.
